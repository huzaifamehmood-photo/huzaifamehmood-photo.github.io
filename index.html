About

I started with a borrowed camera and a habit of standing at the edge of rooms, watching instead of talking.

That habit turned into portraits, into night walks with a tripod, into weddings and convocations where somebody trusted me with a day they only get once.

I led the AKS UET Photography Society for a year and spent most of it convincing people that their eye is worth something before their gear is.
